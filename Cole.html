ABOUT ME



Hey There!! My name is Marcole Balangao but my friends call me Mabs.

I’m a simple guy from Arayat, Pampanga, currently pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering. My primary focus is to specialize in Software Development and Game Design, where I aim to build innovative and engaging digital applications and video games. However, I am also open to exploring Web Development if opportunities arise, as I believe in adapting to industry demands and expanding my skill set to stay versatile in the ever-evolving technological era.

Ever since I was a child I was already interested on Game Designing and Game Development to the point I even studied rescripting and modding several video games such as Pokemon and Minecraft.

I originally pursued a career as a Medical Technologist student but shifted to this field due to several factors, including practicality, technological advancements, and challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020.
